Portland is packed with beautiful locations for engagement photos. Here are some of our favorites:

**Forest Park:** For couples who love nature, Forest Park offers lush greenery and towering trees. The light filtering through the canopy is absolutely magical.

**The Waterfront:** The Willamette River provides stunning backdrops, especially at sunset. We love the area near the Hawthorne Bridge for its mix of urban and natural elements.

**Powell's Books:** For book-loving couples, Powell's City of Books is iconic and quintessentially Portland. The colorful book spines make for vibrant, unique photos.

**The Pearl District:** Cobblestone streets, brick buildings, and modern architecture create an urban vibe that's perfect for stylish couples.

**Mt. Tabor:** This extinct volcano offers panoramic city views, especially beautiful at golden hour. The reservoir and surrounding park provide variety in one location.

**Pittock Mansion:** The mansion grounds offer stunning views of downtown Portland and Mt. Hood. It's a bit of a hike, but the views are worth it.

The best location is one that feels authentic to you as a couple. Where do you love to spend time together? That's where we should shoot.
