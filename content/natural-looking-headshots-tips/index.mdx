Your headshot is often the first impression potential clients or employers have of you. But "professional" doesn't have to mean stiff or boring. Here's how to get headshots that look polished and approachable.

**1. Choose the Right Outfit:** Stick with solid colors that complement your skin tone. Avoid busy patterns or logos. A blazer or button-down shirt works well, but don't be afraid to show personality with a pop of color.

**2. Think About Your Expression:** Practice your smile in the mirror before the session. A genuine smile reaches your eyes—think about something that makes you happy, not just "say cheese."

**3. Posture Matters:** Stand or sit up straight, but keep your shoulders relaxed. Slightly angle your body rather than facing the camera head-on for a more flattering look.

**4. Natural Light is Your Friend:** We shoot headshots using natural light whenever possible. It's softer and more flattering than harsh studio lighting.

**5. Relax and Trust the Process:** The best headshots happen when you're relaxed and comfortable. We'll chat, laugh, and take plenty of shots so you have options to choose from.
