When Emma and Jake reached out to Portland Picture Company, they had one request: keep it real. No forced poses, no stiff formals — just their day, documented honestly. That's exactly what we do.

Their ceremony was held at The Evergreen, one of Portland's most beautiful intimate venues. String lights, old-growth firs, and a guest list of sixty people who genuinely love each other.

## Getting Ready

The morning started with Emma and her bridesmaids in the venue's airy bridal suite. We kept our presence quiet — capturing the nervous energy, the laughter, her mom fastening her dress. These are the frames that make people cry at the gallery reveal.

Jake and his guys were exactly what you'd expect — jokes, coffee, slightly crooked bow ties. We got them sorted.

## The Ceremony

As Emma stepped through the garden doors, Jake's face did exactly what it was supposed to do. We were in three positions before she reached the aisle.

The vows were personal. Handwritten. The kind that make the officiant tear up. We had two cameras running the entire time so nothing was missed.

## Portraits in the Forest

After the ceremony we had thirty minutes with just the two of them in the forest behind the venue. Late afternoon light filtered through the Douglas firs. Emma and Jake were completely relaxed by this point — they danced, they kissed, they forgot we were there.

Those thirty minutes produced half their favorite images from the entire day.

## The Reception

The reception was a proper Portland celebration — craft cocktails, a locally sourced dinner, and a dance floor that never emptied. First dance, father-daughter, toasts that balanced funny and heartfelt.

We stayed until the last song.
